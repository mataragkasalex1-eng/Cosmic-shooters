How to Play & Controls

Objective

Survive waves of asteroids and enemies while maximizing your Points. Small asteroids = +20 Points; Big asteroids = +60 Points. Missed asteroids penalize Points (small −10, big −50).

Core Controls

  • Move: W A S D or Arrow keys — keep moving to avoid falling hazards.
  • Rotate Aim: Q / E — rotate your ship to aim bullets and rockets independently from movement.
  • Shoot: Space — primary weapon uses a clip; press R to manually reload or wait for auto-reload when empty.
  • Rocket: F — fires a consumable rocket (destroys asteroids/does heavy enemy damage).
  • Shield Toggle: 1 — toggle equipped shield (front or full-body) during a run.

Survival Tips

  • Use rotation (Q/E) to aim while strafing with WASD — this lets you dodge and shoot at once.
  • Rockets are scarce — save them for dense clusters or high-value enemies (frigates).
  • When shields are low, disengage and let them recharge; shields recharge after a short delay if not hit.
  • Collect ammo crates and health pickups by ramming them; crates top up reserve ammo, heals restore hull HP.

Upgrades & Currency

  • Points ⇄ SC: Convert 100 Points → 1 SC in Upgrades; you can convert SC back (1 SC → 100 Points).
  • Spend SC on Front Shield, Full-Body Shield, Hull Reinforcement (+20 HP), Engine Tuning (+3 speed), Hull Reinforcement 2, and Rocket charges.
  • Some upgrades are universal (once bought they are available to all ships); others are per-ship—check the Ship modal.

Enemies at a Glance

  • Scouts: Fast, fragile — avoid rams and pick them off.
  • Fighters: Tougher, more damage — prioritize when grouped.
  • Healers/Support: Heal nearby allies — take them out first to stop enemy sustain.
  • Frigates: Heavy enemies with a strong shield and beam — strip shields before hitting hull.

Interface Quick Notes

  • Top-left: Hull Health bar. Top-right: Ammo and Rockets. Menu HUD shows Points and SC.
  • Achievements are claimable in the Achievements panel — unlocked achievements will show a Claim button.
  • Use the Ship modal to buy/select ships; purchases apply immediately if selected.

Tip: The in-game tutorial and encyclopedia provide deeper details — open them from the main menu when ready.

Front Shield
10 SC
A nose-mounted shield that absorbs damage from frontal impacts only. 50 HP; toggled with key "1" when equipped; depletes and then slowly recharge after a short delay. Note: Front Shields are universal — once purchased they are unlocked globally and available for all ships (no need to buy again per ship).
Full-Body Shield
30 SC
Purchase a full-body shield that covers the ship on all sides. 100 HP; toggled with key "1" when equipped; protects from any direction and recharges slowly when offline. Note: Full-Body Shields are universal — once purchased they are unlocked globally and available for all ships (no need to buy again per ship).
Hull Reinforcement
20 SC
Reinforce your hull to increase maximum hull HP by +20 permanently. Increases survivability vs rams and bullets. Costs 20 SC. Purchasing this unlocks Hull Reinforcement 2 (adds +40 max HP) which will become available for 35 SC.
Hull Reinforcement 2
Locked
Powerful hull reinforcement that increases maximum hull HP by +40 permanently. Costs 35 SC. Requires purchasing Hull Reinforcement first to unlock.
Engine Tuning
10 SC
Permanently increases your ship's speed by +3. Costs 10 SC and applies immediately to current and future runs.
Rocket Upgrade
5 SC / piece
Purchase consumable rocket charges (max carry 100). Each rocket costs 5 SC; rockets deal 25 damage to shields and 30 damage to hull when used and will also destroy asteroids in a single hit.
